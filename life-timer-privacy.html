Life Timer Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains the types of personal information that Life Timer collects, the purpose of collection, how it is used, and how it is protected.

1. Types of Personal Information Collected

Life Timer collects the user's birth date and expected date of death. This information is based on user input and does not collect any personally identifiable information.

2. Purpose of Collection

The collected information is used solely for the purpose of calculating and displaying the number of days lived and remaining for the user.

3. Data Protection

Life Timer makes every effort to protect the user's data securely. The collected data is stored in local storage and is not transmitted externally.

4. Changes to the Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as necessary, and any changes will be announced within Life Timer.

5. Contact

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at skykum2004@gmail.com.
